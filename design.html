comfort entities
  • DESIGN Thinking Process

Given the current COVID-19 pandemic, adolescents find themselves in need of a sense of comfort during a stressful time through comfort entities. However, they often fail to recognize what exactly their comfort entities are, and once they do, they tend to self-undermine its importance, feeling the need to “grow out of it.”
